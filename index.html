As part of the promotion of the circular economy and the green economy, the Ministry of Knowledge Economy, Startups and Micro-Enterprises is organizing in partnership with the project "Development of digital and green entrepreneurship in Algeria", funded by the German Federal Ministry for Economic Cooperation and Development and implemented by GIZ, the "GreenTech Algeria 2023" from August 17, 2023 to November 30, 2023.

Greentech
Program

August 17 - November 30, 2023.

The "GreenTech Algeria 2023" program aims to promote green technological entrepreneurship in Algeria. To this end, the program will support project leaders in the creation of new products, processes and services in the following thematic areas:

Waste
management

Reduce waste generation, especially hazardous waste, promote and enhance waste reuse and recycling, and improve waste treatment efficiency.

Sustainable
cities

Promoting sustainability in the urban environment, particularly with regard to the use of sustainable building materials, eco-mobility, and the reduction of air pollution and liquid waste.â€¯

Renewable
energy

Promoting the production, storage and access to renewable energies, in particular decentralized solar energy production and the conversion of renewable energies into green hydrogen.

Environmental
protection

Development of cleaner production technologies to prevent and mitigate negative impacts on the environment, in particular those damaging biodiversity

Mitigation and adaptation
to climate change

Development of innovative solutions to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, including through increased resource efficiency, particularly in export value chains, or to help households and businesses adapt to climate change.

The GreenTech Algeria 2023 support program consists of two parts:
Phase one: an intensive 4-day practical training program (the bootcamp).
Phase two: a 4-week technical support program, at the end of which the participating teams will present their solutions to a jury of experts who will select the winners.
